About

01

Between tradition
and the present moment

David Gabatashvili is a Georgian painter whose work navigates the tension between classical technique and contemporary sensibility. Trained in the figurative traditions of Tbilisi and later in the ateliers of Florence, his canvases carry the memory of both worlds.

Each work is a quiet negotiation — between light and shadow, between the visible and the merely felt. He paints slowly, deliberately, as one who understands that restraint is its own form of eloquence.
